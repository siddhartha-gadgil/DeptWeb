Symposium on Harmonic Analysis

On the occasion of the superannuation of Prof. S. Thangavelu

Tuesday, 26 July 2022

 

A hybrid symposium on Harmonic Analysis will be organized by the Department of Mathematics, Indian Institute of Science on Tuesday, 26 July 2022 on the occasion of the superannuation of Prof. S. Thangavelu.

This is part of a three-day symposium on the occasion of superannuation of three of our faculty members.

Invited Speakers

  • Bernhard Krotz (University of Paderborn, Germany)
  • Jotsaroop Kaur (IISER Mohali)
  • Rahul Garg (IISER Bhopal)
  • Pritam Ganguly (IISc)
