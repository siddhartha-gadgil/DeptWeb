Dirichlet integral and p-Dirichlet integral: Sobolev spaces, existence
of minimizers, weak form of the Euler-Lagrange equations.

* Linear Regularity: `$L^2$` estimate, Schauder estimates, `$L^p$` estimates
* DeGiorgi-Nash-Moser theory: Moser iteration, Harnack inequality,
DeGiorgi’s solution of Hilbert’s 19th problem.
* Regularity of `$p$`-Laplacian: `$C^{1,\alpha}$` regularity and Uhlenbeck
structure.
