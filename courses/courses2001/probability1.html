PROBABILITY THEORY

Probability measures and randown variables, p and l systems, expectation, moment generating function, characteristic function, laws  of large numbers, limit theorems, conditional contribution and expectation, martingales, infinitely divisible laws and stable laws.

Books

  • Breiman, L., Probability, Addison-Wesley, 1968.

  • Laha, R. G., and Rohatgi, V. K., Probability Theory, Jhon Wiley, 1979.

  • Borkar, V. S., Probability theory: An Advanced Course, Springer, 1995.
