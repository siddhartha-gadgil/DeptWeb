In this talk, we present some natural crystallizations of the generalized lens spaces $L(p, q_1, \dots, q_n)$ for integers $p\geq 2$, $n\geq 1$ and integers $q_1, \dots, q_n$
relatively prime to $p$. These crystallizations are quotients of triangulations of the sphere $S^{2n+1}$.
