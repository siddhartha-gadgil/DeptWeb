From classical results on the special values of Rankin-Selberg `$L$`-functions attached to elliptic modular cusp forms, it follows that the ratios of successive special values are algebraic numbers. In this talk, I will sketch a proof showing that, under suitable assumptions, congruent cusp forms give rise to congruent ratios of successive special values of Rankin-Selberg `$L$`-functions attached to them.
